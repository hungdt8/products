A complete toolkit for managing your contacts, right from your pocket.

One-Tap Backup

Export all your contacts instantly with a single tap. Your backup includes names, phone numbers, emails, and organizations.

VCF & CSV Formats

Choose between vCard (.vcf) for universal device compatibility or CSV (.csv) for spreadsheet-friendly exports.

Find Duplicates

Scan your contact list to detect duplicate entries. Review grouped duplicates and remove them with one tap to keep your list clean.
