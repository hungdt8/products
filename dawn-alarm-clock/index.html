Questions

Good to know.

How does the 15-day trial work?

Every feature is unlocked automatically for your first 15 days — no credit card, no sign-up. When the trial ends, you can unlock the full version with a one-time purchase. If you skip the purchase, Dawn still works for core alarm use, but premium features will ask to unlock.

Does Dawn work if I force-quit the app?

Yes. Alarms use iOS local notifications, so they fire even when the app isn't running. The chain scheduler ensures your alarm rings continuously for up to 15 minutes — plenty of time even for heavy sleepers.

Will alarms ring on silent / Do Not Disturb?

Yes. Dawn uses time-sensitive notifications with custom sounds, which iOS allows through silent mode and Focus. Make sure to grant notification permission on first launch — that's the only permission needed.

Do you collect any data?

No. Dawn has no account system, no analytics, and no third-party SDKs. Your alarms, settings, and purchase status live entirely on your device. Weather uses Apple WeatherKit and never leaves Apple's servers.

Why a one-time purchase and not a subscription?

An alarm clock doesn't need monthly server costs. Charging you forever for something that wakes you up would be weird. Pay once, own it forever — that's how software used to work, and for Dawn it still does.

Can I use Dawn on my iPad too?

Yes. Dawn is a universal app — one purchase covers iPhone and iPad. The bedside clock mode looks especially lovely on iPad.
