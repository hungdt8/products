How it works

1

Move Milo

Swipe anywhere on the board, or tap the on-screen arrows. Milo walks one square per move in any direction.

2

Push the boxes

Walk Milo into a box to push it forward. You can only push one box at a time — and you can never pull. Plan your route.

3

Fill every goal

Get every terracotta box onto a glowing goal marker. When all goals are filled, the level is solved. Stuck? Undo anytime.
