Frequently asked

Quietly answered.

Does Money Tracker connect to my bank?

No, and that’s on purpose. You decide what gets logged. We never ask for bank credentials, never scrape statements, and never see your money move. Most people find that, after a few days, manually logging takes about thirty seconds — and you remember a lot more about where it went.

Where is my data stored?

On your device, first and foremost. If you turn on iCloud sync, the data is end-to-end encrypted with a key only you hold. We don’t have a server that can read it.

Can I track in multiple currencies?

Yes — Money Tracker supports 180 currencies, including crypto, with mid-market rates refreshed daily. Hold as many as you like, side by side.

Is it really a one-time purchase?

Yes. One payment of $1.99 unlocks the full version forever — every feature, on every device you own, including all future updates. No subscription, no upsells, nothing that quietly renews.

Which devices does it run on?

Money Tracker is on iPhone and iPad today, with a Mac companion in the works. Your data syncs cleanly between them via your own iCloud account.
